# West Coast United States Cuisine

## Regional Overview
Pacific Coast cooking emphasizing fresh, seasonal ingredients, health-conscious preparations, and multicultural fusion. From California's farm-to-table movement to Pacific Northwest seafood traditions, West Coast cuisine celebrates innovation while respecting ingredient quality.

## Cuisine Characteristics
- Farm-to-table and seasonal cooking
- Asian-Pacific fusion influences
- Abundant fresh produce year-round
- Sustainable seafood emphasis
- Health-conscious preparations
- Mexican-California crossover
- Wine country pairings
- Sourdough bread traditions

## Representative Dishes
- California-style salads and bowls
- Fish tacos and ceviche
- Pacific salmon preparations
- Dungeness crab dishes
- Sourdough bread and artisan baking
- Avocado-forward dishes
- Asian fusion bowls and plates

## Recipe Guidelines
All recipes in this folder must follow the [FlavorAtlas_Recipe_Template.md](../FlavorAtlas_Recipe_Template.md) format and include:
