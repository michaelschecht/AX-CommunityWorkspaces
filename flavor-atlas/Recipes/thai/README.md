# Thai Cuisine

## Regional Overview
Thai cuisine is renowned for balancing five fundamental flavors: sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and umami (savory), with spicy heat adding another dimension. Regional variations span from Northern Thai (khao soi) to Southern (massaman curry) to Central (pad thai) to Northeastern Isaan cuisine.

## Cuisine Characteristics
- Balance of five flavors in every dish
- Fresh herbs: Thai basil, cilantro, lemongrass, galangal
- Curry pastes built from scratch
- Coconut milk-based and tamarind-based dishes
- Fish sauce and shrimp paste for umami depth
- Wok cooking and high-heat techniques
- Rice and noodles as foundations

## Representative Dishes
- Pad Thai and other stir-fried noodles
- Green, red, yellow, and massaman curries
- Tom yum and tom kha soups
- Som tam (papaya salad)
- Larb and other Isaan specialties
- Pad krapow (holy basil stir-fry)
- Mango sticky rice
