# Mediterranean Cuisine

## Regional Overview
Mediterranean cuisine encompasses the culinary traditions of countries bordering the Mediterranean Sea: Greece, Italy, Spain, Southern France, Turkey, Lebanon, Morocco, and others. Characterized by olive oil, fresh vegetables, seafood, whole grains, and aromatic herbs, this cuisine is celebrated for both flavor and health benefits.

## Cuisine Characteristics
- Olive oil as primary fat
- Fresh vegetables, legumes, and whole grains
- Seafood and moderate amounts of poultry
- Fresh and dried herbs: oregano, thyme, rosemary, parsley
- Citrus and vinegar for brightness
- Tomatoes, eggplant, peppers as foundations
- Simple preparations highlighting ingredient quality

## Representative Dishes
- Greek: Moussaka, spanakopita, souvlaki
- Italian: Pasta dishes, risotto, osso buco
- Spanish: Paella, gazpacho, tapas
- Middle Eastern: Hummus, falafel, shawarma, tabbouleh
- Turkish: Kebabs, mezes, baklava
- Moroccan: Tagines, couscous, b'stilla
